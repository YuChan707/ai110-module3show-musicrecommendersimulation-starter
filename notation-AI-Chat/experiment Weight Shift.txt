It has no genre match at all, but its mood + close energy (0.76 vs target 0.8) now outweighs a genre match from a song with mediocre energy. This is exactly the bias correction the experiment was designed to test.

Potential side effects to be aware of
Energy over-prioritization. Energy now accounts for up to 50% of the max score (2.0 / 4.0). Songs with energy close to the target but completely wrong genre and mood can still rank in the top 5 — ranks 5 and 6 above ("Night Drive Loop", "Electronic Pulse") have no genre or mood match at all and still score 1.9.

Genre match is now worth the same as mood match. The original design intentionally made genre the strongest signal. At 1.0 : 1.0, genre and mood are peers — if you want genre to still lead, consider a middle value like 1.5 rather than the full halving to 1.0.
